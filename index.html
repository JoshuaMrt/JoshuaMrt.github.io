About

Quarantine due to COVID-19 is the reason why people not allowed to go outside their houses and referred to buying their needs and wants online. Online shopping is one of the commonly used mediums for convenient shopping. In fact, it is a popular means of shopping among the internet community today. Anything else like dress, gadgets, or pet trend is getting more prevalent with each passing day in online shopping. Many websites and applications are created every year to fill the rising request of comfortable shopping patterns. In the Philippines, few websites and online stores were created. However, they do not have other functions such as a mapping system. This research aims to develop a plant selling website with a mapping system using the advanced technology. Some customers want to track and know how long they will wait for their orders to arrive that is why this website will feature the real time distance of the delivery to their houses. Also, it will be easier for the sellers of plants to identify the exact location of their customers. This website will give a big relief to the sellers and buyers as it will be more convenient for them to sell and buy different plants online.
